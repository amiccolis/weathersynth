WeatherSynth

WeatherSynth is a web-based synthesizer with no knobs - it changes based on the weather. Temperature, humidity, current conditions, wind speed, and cloud cover all have a hand in how the sound is generated. Move the marker to different locations to have the weather play the synthesizer.

Powered by Forecast.io.

Put on the map using Mapbox.

Synthesized by the Web Audio API.

Made by Andy Miccolis for Music Hack Day NYC 2013.
